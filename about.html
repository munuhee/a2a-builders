About A2A Builders

Founded in 2023 by Eng. Amos Kiarie

Our Story

A2A Builders, based in Mombasa Nyali, was established in 2023 by Eng. Amos Kiarie. We are dedicated to providing exceptional construction services, utilizing innovative techniques and unwavering commitment to quality.

  • Committed to excellence and customer satisfaction
  • Utilizing cutting-edge construction methods
  • Delivering projects on time and within budget

Our mission is to exceed expectations and build lasting relationships with our clients, partners, and communities. We strive for innovation, sustainability, and integrity in every project we undertake.

We offer various services such as Drawing of Architectural plans, structural design, interior design, general construction works, and Renovation works.
